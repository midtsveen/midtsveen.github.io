About Me

I enjoy taking walks in the city center, watching the world go by and observing people. Music is a big part of my life - I love progressive psytrance and metal. Computers fascinate me, and I'm interested in technology.

As for dislikes, I'm not a fan of traveling or long car rides. I'm skeptical about electric cars like Teslas - the idea of computers on wheels doesn't sit well with me. TVs are another no-go; the constant audio makes me feel sick and tired.

I'm a member of Innovasjon- og teknologipartiet in Norway. The party stands for:

  • Securing freedom of thought and expression by preventing censorship and surveillance from both public and private actors.
  • Full transparency in government administration, providing access to all relevant information for citizens to make informed decisions and perform necessary oversight.
  • Protecting academic freedom and opposing censorship of research results, with increased funding for research environments and open access to research results and data.
  • Changing drug policy from a criminal justice approach to a health policy approach, including decriminalization of possession and use of user doses.

These principles align with my beliefs in digital rights, transparency, and evidence-based policy-making.
