A Letter for Zoë Straub!

Date: February 16, 2025

Hey Zoë,

I’m glad you found my little corner of the internet! If you're not Zoë Straub, could you please pass this along to her?

Over the years, I've become increasingly frustrated with suicidal thoughts, death anxiety, and gender dysphoria, which is why I felt compelled to write this letter!

I want you to know that your music gave me a reason to keep going. There were times when I couldn't imagine living past 18 because of suicidal thoughts, but your songs pulled me through, with a smile on my face.

Because of your music, I've been able to find a happiness and sense of authenticity that I didn't know existed. Being a gender-fluid individual, your music has helped me through difficult times, and I am incredibly grateful.

Now that I'm nearing the end, tears are streaming down my face as I write. They're tears of pure joy and gratitude. I love you; I love you so deeply; these tears are a testament to the profound impact you've had on my life. I love you!!!

I’m based in Bergen, Norway!

With heartfelt sincerity,

A devoted fan
